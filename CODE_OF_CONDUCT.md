# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in
KB Factory a harassment-free, welcoming, and respectful experience for everyone,
regardless of background or identity. We pledge to act and interact in ways that
contribute to an open, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standards

We expect everyone in the project to:

- be respectful, considerate, and welcoming;
- assume good faith and give constructive feedback gracefully;
- focus on what is best for the community and the project;
- show empathy toward other community members.

Behavior that harasses, demeans, or excludes others is not tolerated.

## Adoption by reference

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**. The full text —
including the complete list of unacceptable behaviors, enforcement
responsibilities, scope, and the tiered enforcement guidelines — is the
authoritative standard for this community and is available at:

- <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

By participating, you agree to uphold that document in full.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and
will take appropriate, fair corrective action in response to behavior they deem
inappropriate.

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported **privately to the project
maintainer, [@regismvargas](https://github.com/regismvargas), through GitHub**
(for example, via the contact details on their GitHub profile). Reports will be
reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and the privacy and security of
the reporter will be respected.
